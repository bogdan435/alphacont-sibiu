# Accounting for PFA in Sibiu

A sole trader in Sibiu needs a clear record of income, expenses, and supporting documents. Good organization reduces mistakes and helps meet tax deadlines.

## What you should know

A PFA activity should be tracked constantly, not only before filing tax forms. Documents should be stored in order and reviewed regularly.

## Useful documents or steps

- issued and received invoices
- receipts and bank statements
- monthly summary of income and expenses

## Conclusion

Well-organized accounting helps a sole trader avoid problems and make better decisions.
