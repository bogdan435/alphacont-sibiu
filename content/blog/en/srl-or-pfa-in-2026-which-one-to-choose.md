# SRL or PFA in 2026 - which one should you choose?

If you are starting a new activity, one of the most important questions is whether to choose an SRL or a PFA. The right choice depends on your activity type, expected volume, and how you want to manage taxes and risk.

## When a PFA can make more sense

A PFA can be simpler for independent activity, with a lighter structure and fewer documents. It is often chosen when the business is just starting and costs need to stay under control.

## When an SRL can make more sense

An SRL usually offers more flexibility for growth, larger collaborations, and a clearer separation between the company and the individual. It often fits better when you want to grow, hire, or handle larger volumes.

## What to review before deciding

- what level of income you estimate in the first year
- how simple or complex the activity will be
- whether you will have employees or collaborators
- how important it is to limit personal risk

## Conclusion

There is no single best answer for everyone. Choosing between SRL and PFA should be based on activity, taxes, and growth plans, and an accountant can help you make that choice more clearly from the start.
