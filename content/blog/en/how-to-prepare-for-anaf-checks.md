# How to prepare for ANAF checks or reviews

Tax reviews are easier to handle when the company documents are organized and up to date.

## What you should know

Preparation does not start on the day of the review. It starts with keeping clear records and maintaining constant communication with the accounting side.

## Useful documents or steps

- archive supporting documents properly
- review contracts and employee records
- keep a clear record of invoices and payments

## Conclusion

An organized business can respond more easily and with less stress during a review.
