# Payroll for small employers

For small businesses, payroll should be accurate, predictable, and properly documented. Even small mistakes can create confusion and unnecessary costs.

## What you should know

Payroll is not only about salary payment. It also includes employee records, correct calculations, and related reporting obligations.

## Useful documents or steps

- updated employment contracts
- leave and absence records
- checking payroll statements before submission

## Conclusion

A well-organized payroll process helps the business operate more safely and efficiently.
