# How much does an accountant cost in Sibiu?

One of the most common questions is how much an accountant costs in Sibiu. The honest answer is that the price depends on document volume, company type, and the level of support you actually need.

## What influences the price

The cost for a sole trader with simple activity will be different from that of an LLC with employees, payroll, and extra obligations. It also matters whether you need only records and filings or also payroll, tax clarification, and inspection support.

## What to ask before choosing

- whether the offer includes filings and monthly communication
- whether payroll and personnel documents are priced separately
- how documents are sent and how fast you get a reply
- whether support exists for RO e-Factura, SAF-T, or ANAF inspections
