# What taxes does an LLC pay in Romania?

An LLC in Romania can have several types of tax obligations depending on its tax regime, employee count, and activity profile. That is why it matters to know in advance what comes next and which documents must be prepared.

## The obligations that appear most often

In practice, an LLC tracks the taxes that apply to the company, payroll taxes if there are employees, and periodic filings. In addition, there can also be VAT, RO e-Factura, or other reporting obligations specific to the activity.

## Why problems appear

Problems usually appear when the company does not know exactly which taxes are due, which documents are missing, or which deadline matters next. Lack of clarity leads to delays, corrections, and unnecessary stress.

## What helps in practice

- a clear calendar of tax deadlines
- monthly communication with the accountant
- documents sent in an organized and timely way
- clarification before important decisions are made

## Conclusion

An LLC needs more than filing obligations. It needs real financial control. With proper records and reliable accounting support, taxes and deadlines become much easier to manage.
